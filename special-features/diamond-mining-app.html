Diamond Mining App
When the Diamond Mining function is unlocked, players can mine $DML every 3 hours.
  • Default productivity: 18 $DML/3 hours
  • Productivity boosting when locking NFTs depends on the value of NFT
  • Maximum lock-up is 3 different NFTs
  • The values of the NFT can be added together to increase the x% of boost
NFT is the key to being able to use the Mining app. Players need to own at least 1 NFT to activate the $DML mining feature on the diamond mining app.
  • Simple, intuitive interface
  • Unlock the app with NFT
  • Lock NFTs to increase mining productivity
  • Play mini-games and get bonuses
